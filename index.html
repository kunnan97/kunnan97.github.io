oneNUS

Previous Next

oneNUS is a project under the Orbital Programme 2020 by NUS School of Computing(SOC). This is my first software development project collaborated with another developer. oneNUS is a navigation mobile application aim to provide NUS students, staff and visitors with better route suggestions when navigating within the vicinities of NUS. We realised that this is a problem in NUS as navigation options such as Google Maps do not take NUS's internal shuttle bus into consideration. Some parts of the school frequent by students and staff will need NUS's internal shuttle bus to reach. Other than navigation, we provide location identifier to users , showing nearby facilities such as Automated Teller Machines(ATMs), convenience stores and supermarkets etc.
My main responsibility is building the user interface, integrating APIs such as Google Places and Google Location for location features and fetching of data from Firebase.
Our prototype acheived the Second Highest Level of Achievement Apollo 11 under Orbital Programme 2020, NUS School of Computing.
